Business Use Cases

Inventory System


Business Use Case Name:
OrderEdibleSupplies
Short Description:
This use case enables the Purchasing manager to order edible cannabis supplies from supplier .
Actors:
PurchasingManager, SupplierAgent
Trigger
Starbuds would like to increase/replenish inventory of cannabis products.
Result:
Ordered inventory of edible cannabis products.
Business Use Case Name:
OrderNonEdibleSupplies
Short Description:
This use case enables the Purchasing Manager to order non-edible cannabis supplies from supplier .
Actors:
PurchasingManager, SupplierAgent
Trigger
Starbuds would like to increase/replenish inventory of cannabis products.
Result:
Non-edible cannabis products are ordered.
Business Use Case Name:
SendPayment
Short Description:
This use case enables the purchasing manager to send the payment for the supplies to the supplier.
Actors:
PurchasingManager, Supplier
Trigger:
Inventory has space that needs to be filled up with proper items.
Result:
Payment to the supplier is made.
Business Use Case Name:
RecieveInvoice
Short Description:
The supplier sends back the invoice for the purchasing order to the company.
Actors:
PurchasingManager,Supplier
Trigger:
Supplier recives the payment for the items requested.
Result:
Receipt is recived.
Business Use Case Name:
ReportTransaction
Short Description:
The purchasing manager will report any financial transaction made to the book keeper who keeps track of the payments.
Actors:
PurchasingManager, BookKeeper
Trigger:
Purchasing manager makes a financial transaction and wants to report it.
Result:
BookKeeper is made aware of the transaction.
Business Use Case Name:
RecordTransaction
Short Description:
BookKeeper records the transactions on a company database.
Actors:
BookKeeper
Trigger:
BookKeeper is made aware of a transaction that needs to be recorded.
Result:
A record for the transaction is made.
Business Use Case Name:
ReceiveInventorySupplies
Short Description:
This use case enables the stock clerk to receive inventory from the supplier.
Actors:
StoreClerk, SupplierAgent
Trigger
Supplier schedules and delivers the items
Result:
Received inventory supplies.
Business Use Case Name:
CheckRecivedSupplies
Short Description:
This use case enables the stock clerk to inspect the supplies and make sure they are not damaged or are exactly what was requested.
Actors:
StockClerk
Trigger:
Stock clerk recives supplies from the supplier agent.
Result:
Goods are inspected before being added to the inventory.
Business Use Case Name:
UpdateInventory
Short Description:
This use case enables the stock clerk to update the store inventory for the supplies recived.
Actors:
StockClerk
Trigger:
Stock clerk recived supplies from a supplier agent.
Result:
Items are added to the inventory.
